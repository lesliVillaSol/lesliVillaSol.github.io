Support Animal Sancturaries

According to "The Consumer's Guide to Effective Environmental Choices", consumers should prioritize addressing the most harmful activities: car/travel, meat consumption, household construction, and household water use. Another way to get involved as a consumer, is to support animal sancturaries through donations, purchases or volunteer work.
